## The RESONATE Protocol

### 00 , Session Lifecycle (Harness)

**Before doing anything else**, read `AGENTS.md` and `progress.md` (if it exists) to understand the current execution state. You must update `progress.md` with your status before ending the session.

### N , Node

Identify the individual. What is their structural and informal position?
Map their MINE state and their stated goals.

### E , Exchange

Determine what flows between nodes. Is it information, capital, or favors?
Track the transactional baseline.

### T , Trust

Evaluate the reliability of the node. Do they deliver on promises?
Measure historical consistency.

### W , Worth

Assess the value of the relationship to the broader system.
What unique capability does this connection unlock?

### O , Obligation

Identify the debts. Who owes what to whom?
Map the unspoken expectations.

### R , Reciprocity

Analyze the balance of trade. Is the relationship parasitic or symbiotic?
Look for mutual elevation.

### K , Kinship

Determine the depth of alignment. Do they share the same 'Soul' layer?
Identify if this is a purely transactional link or a foundational alliance.

# /resonate , Map Harmonic Alliances

> *"Your power is bottlenecked by the rigidity of your grid."*

**RESONATE** is the third verb in the PERSON OS pipeline. It converts the abstract concept of "networking" into a physical grid of load-bearing relationships, each mapped with precise exchange mechanics, trust trajectories, and future velocity vectors. It is not a contact list. It is a political physics model.

**Pipeline position**: VERB 3 of 6
**Reads**: `profile/CORE.md`, `experience/*.md` (REQUIRED)
**Writes to**: `resonate/[person-or-node].md`
**Previous step**: `/experience`
**Next step**: `/strategise`

---

## Usage

```
/resonate
```

---

## Step 1: Pre-Reasoning Governance (PASS) → HUMAN APPROVAL REQUIRED

Before starting, restate the user's intent back to them using the PASS framework:

- **Purpose**: Map the external nodes that scale the user's capability in harmonic resonance. We are looking for load-bearing relationships, not peripheral contacts.
- **Anti-Purpose**: Do NOT accept a simple list of friends, colleagues, or LinkedIn connections. We only want relationships where value is actively flowing in both directions and where the node materially affects the user's GROWTH vector.
- **Success-Signal**: Each node entry passes the "Remove Test" (if this person disappeared from the user's network tomorrow, would it create a measurable gap in their capability or trajectory?).
- **Synthesis**: Transforms abstract networks into physical leverage (`resonate/[node].md`) that will feed directly into `/strategise`.

Present PASS table and ask: *"Do you approve this execution intent?"*
**STOP AND WAIT FOR EXPLICIT HUMAN APPROVAL.**

*(Execute ONLY after PASS approval)*

- **Source**: `experience/*.md` (to understand who has appeared in their milestones, surface latent relationships)

Surface: "Based on your profile as [Identity], I am looking for the 4-5 people currently making or breaking your trajectory."

## Step 3: Multi-Round Interrogation

**Opening probe:** *"Who are the 4-5 people your career most depends on right now? Not just people you respect. People where removing them from your life would create a real capability or opportunity gap."*

**Depth probes per node** (run these if the initial answer is shallow):
1. *"What do you give this person that they cannot easily get elsewhere?"*
2. *"What do they give you that you genuinely cannot replace?"*
3. *"When was the last moment this relationship deepened? And when was the last moment you felt friction with them?"*
4. *"If this person had to vouch for you to an important room you have never entered, would they? Why or why not?"*
5. *"What is the unspoken obligation in this relationship? What would happen if you called in a favor today?"*

---

## Step 4: Synthesis Framework (NETWORK)

Synthesize each node into a Relationship Ledger entry using the **NETWORK** framework. Target: 40-70 words per letter.

- **[N]ode** (The entity): Full name, current role, company, and their specific domain of influence. Name their power in one sentence.
- **[E]xchange** (The value trade): Be specific and asymmetric. What exact product, intelligence, access, or credibility do you provide? What exact product, intelligence, access, or credibility do they provide back? Name the actual assets, not categories.
- **[T]rust** (The current state): Describe the trust trajectory. Is it rising, stable, or fraying? Identify the most recent moment that defined the current trust level. Include any unresolved tension.
- **[W]orth** (The geopolitical leverage): What room does this person open for you that you cannot access alone? Name the specific gate they hold.
- **[O]bligation** (What is owed): Explicitly name any existing debt, promise, or implicit expectation in the relationship, on both sides.
- **[R]esolve** (The required action): The specific next move to either strengthen this relationship, resolve friction, or convert a dormant node into an active one. Concrete action, not aspiration.
- **[K]inematics** (Future velocity): If this relationship strengthens, what specific capability or opportunity unlocks? If it weakens, what specific constraint does it create?

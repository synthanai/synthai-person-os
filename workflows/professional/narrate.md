## The NARRATE Protocol

### 00 , Session Lifecycle (Harness)

**Before doing anything else**, read `AGENTS.md` and `progress.md` (if it exists) to understand the current execution state. You must update `progress.md` with your status before ending the session.

### S , Story

Craft the foundational hook. What is the human element?
Begin with a specific, relatable scenario.

### O , Operations

Explain the mechanics. How does it actually work?
Move from the 'why' to the 'how'. Provide concrete details.

### C , Contrast

Highlight the delta. How is this different from the status quo?
Polarize the narrative. Show the old way versus the new way.

### I , Instruction

Provide actionable value. What can the audience do right now?
Give away the secret. Generosity builds authority.

### A , Adversarial

Anticipate and neutralize the counter-argument.
Address the skeptic directly. Steelman their concern and dismantle it.

### L , Legacy

Tie the specific message back to your overarching CORE.
Ensure this single narrative reinforces your long-term positioning.

### 💾 Mandatory Persist (State Handoff)

> **CRITICAL RULE**: Do not rely on chat context to pass state to the next workflow. 

Every workflow execution MUST persist its output to a physical artifact in the repository. This artifact serves as the uncorrupted input for the next verb in the sequence. 

- **If an appropriate directory exists**, save it there (e.g., `operations/spar/`, `2-research/`).
- **If no directory is defined**, save it to `.agent/scratch/` or `operations/queues/` with a descriptive timestamped filename.

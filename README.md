## What Is This?

Person-OS is an open cognitive framework that maps how individuals and organizations operate across three fundamental dimensions:

| Dimension | Domain | Question It Answers |
|-----------|--------|---------------------|
| **Ontology** | Anatomy of Awareness | What are the layers of consciousness? (Mind, Body, Soul, Spirit, Aura) |
| **Epistemology** | Anatomy of Reasoning | How does knowledge move? (Potent, Talent, Fluent, Latent) |
| **Praxeology** | Anatomy of Contribution | How does action manifest? (12 Acro-Verbs in 3 orbits) |

## The ARC TORUS

The core geometry of Person-OS is the **ARC TORUS**: a circular architecture of 12 cognitive verbs organized into 3 orbits.

### Awareness Orbit (Intake)
`STEAL` → `LEARN` → `PROBE` → `REMIND`

### Reasoning Orbit (Compression)
`DIGEST` → `SPARKIT` → `GATHER` → `COMPARE`

### Contribution Orbit (Output)
`POSTURE` → `FORGE` → `TEACH` → `MENTOR`

## Philosophy

> *"An organization is alive. We must nurture its Mind, Body, and Soul to keep it alive."*

The TORUS-MINE framework is grounded in the conviction that both individuals and organizations are living systems. The MBS (Mind-Body-Soul) ontology provides the awareness layer. The MINE (MINE Is Not Extraction) matrix provides the reasoning layer. The ARC TORUS provides the contribution layer.
